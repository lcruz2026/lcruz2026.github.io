My Fan-made Stands

About the stands I made for fun

(Stands are basically manifestations that represents someone's mind/fighting spirit. You can think of them as powers represented using their own character)

1.『Fireflies』- The stand can release "spores" through its palms and mouth. The "spores" are also released through punching.
The spores are actually eggs that eventually hatch into firefly-like creatures. The "fireflies"" bite anything they land on.
When bitten by the "fireflies", it will cause a paralysis effect and the more times you get bit, the longer the effect lasts
until the target is fully paralyzed. When they bite the user howver, it will give them energy. The user can gain so much energy that they
won't be able to sleep but fatigue is still felt. The "spores" are easily moved by wind but they will stick to the first surface they land on.

2.『Mad IQs』- This Stand causes the target to slowly lose their sense of pain and eventually their sense of touch.
Ability activates when the user makes contact with the target multiple times in the same spot

3.『Two Moons』 - The user can wear 1 of 2 "Moon Suits", one heavily focuses into offense and one leans heavily into defense.
The User can set the Defensive Suit to a target wherein the suit will protect the target. When this happens, the user can switch places with the suit at will.

4.『Inside of my Heart』- This stand is bound to a key that can open any door thathas a keyhole. when a door is opened using the stand,
it transforms the room. The interior is completely up to the user. Only one room can be changed at a time.

5.『Soul Sister』- The stand manifests as shapeshifting black ink. Has the power to look into the target's mind.
This abiltiy can be used to gather information from the target which appears as black text along the walls. The ability is also used to inflict fear.
